About me

I am a highly personable and enthusiastic career changer who thrives on problem solving and collaboration, with a focus on building full stack web applications using JavaScript, React, Express and using RESTful API methods.

After a successful career as a graphic designer and project manager, I realised I needed a change. My choice of coding and software development was influenced by childhood nostalgia of tinkering with oscilloscopes and circuit boards, along with an love of STEM subjects and puzzle-solving.

During my time as an Junior Instructor at CodeClan, I found genuine satisfaction in melding my own learning with student learning. I embraced the challenge of researching and conveying complex concepts into bite-sized, original and understandable lessons. My ability to help students grow their self-confidence was vital for their success. Every day was unique, whether it was mentoring or collaborating with students and colleagues, or increasing my knowledge to design scalable and maintainable web applications. The dynamic environment constantly exposed me to new technologies and resources, driving my self-development.

Now, I am ready to transition into an industry role, where I am eager to expand on my knowledge, infuse positivity and energy into a team, and flourish in the software industry.
